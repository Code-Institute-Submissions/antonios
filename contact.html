Where We Are

By Car

Easy access on the M27 (junction 11) or A27 from Portsmouth or Southampton. There’s plenty of parking in the town centre but don’t miss the pay-and-display right behind Antonio’s.


By Train

Plenty of trains stop at Fareham rail station, Antonio’s is an easy 15-minute walk away.


By Bus

Frequent services to Fareham bus station, Antonio’s is a five-minute walk away.
