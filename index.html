Our Story

Here at Antonio’s, we pride ourselves in offering our customers an authentic Spanish experience, and our attentive and friendly service will make your visit one to remember.


Our menus offer one of the largest selections of any restaurant, and there really is something for everyone. The recipes originate from various regions of Spain, and all the food is freshly prepared in house. The wine that accompanies the meals are sourced directly from Spanish vineyards, which ensures that you will taste some of the more unusual and hard to fine wines.
