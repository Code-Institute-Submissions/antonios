Our Story

Antonio’s is a Spanish restaurant in the heart of Fareham that serves a traditional Spanish cuisine with plenty of weekly specials.


For over 30 years working it the hospitality industry Antonio has developed a passion for customer satisfaction through great food and fine wines, which is reflected in the personal service you receive.
