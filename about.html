Meet The Team

Antonio Pego Pineiro | Proprietor

Tony started his career in hospitality in Cedeira, Galicia in northwestern Spain at the age of 14 and came to England when he was 20. He has worked in many restaurants in Reading, Newbury and Southampton. He opened Antonio’s in December 2004.


Zara Pego Pineiro | Proprietress

Zara started her career in hospitality at the age if 18, working part-time at Whitbread in The Cormorant pub, Portchester. She has held her Personal Licence since the age of 21. Her last pub was the Red Lion, Stubbington. She left the pub industry in December 2004.


Diego | Head Chef & Partner

Diego is the head chef and has been part of Antonio’s team for two years, from Galicia, becoming partner 2021.


David | Second Chef

David is the second chef from the south of Spain and has been part of Antonio’s team for one year.
